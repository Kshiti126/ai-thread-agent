The problem is to design a system that can automatically 
generate structured and engaging Twitter/X threads from a simple 
user-provided topic. 
The target users include content creators, students, and 
professionals who want to share ideas on social media but lack 
the time to research, structure, and write threads effectively. 
The need arises because creating high-quality threads involves 
multiple steps such as planning the content, gathering relevant 
information, structuring it logically, and writing it in an engaging 
format. 
A single-step LLM solution is insufficient for this task because it 
requires multiple stages of reasoning and tool usage. Therefore, 
an agentic approach is suitable, where multiple specialized 
agents collaborate to perform planning, research, and content 
generation.
